Our Story

Founded in 2009, PropertyPro Management began with a simple mission: to make property ownership stress-free and profitable for property owners while providing exceptional rental experiences for tenants.

Over the past 15 years, we've grown from a small local company to one of the region's most trusted property management firms, managing over 500 properties and serving more than 1,000 satisfied clients.

Our success is built on three core principles: transparency, reliability, and exceptional service. We believe that property management is not just about collecting rent‚Äîit's about building lasting relationships and creating value for everyone involved.
