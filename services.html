Frequently Asked Questions

How do you screen tenants?

+

We conduct comprehensive background checks including credit score analysis, employment verification, income verification, criminal background checks, and previous landlord references. We require tenants to have a minimum credit score of 650 and income of at least 3 times the monthly rent.

How quickly do you respond to maintenance requests?

+

Emergency maintenance requests are handled within 24 hours. Non-emergency requests are typically addressed within 72 hours. We have a 24/7 emergency hotline for urgent issues like plumbing leaks, electrical problems, or security concerns.

When do I receive rental income?

+

Rental income is distributed to property owners by the 15th of each month via direct deposit, along with a detailed financial statement. If rent is collected late, payment is made within 3 business days of collection.

What happens if a tenant doesn't pay rent?

+

We have a strict rent collection policy with late fees applied after the grace period. If payment issues persist, we initiate the eviction process while keeping you informed every step of the way. We handle all legal proceedings and court appearances.
